Chocolate Tapioca
½ cup sugar
3 tbsp minute tapioca
3 cups milk
1 egg, well beaten
1 tsp vanilla

mix chocolate:
½ cup cocoa
½ cup sugar
3 tbsp butter

Mix sugar, tapioca, milk, egg, chocolate in medium saucepan; let stand 5 minutes.

Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to FULL boil.
Remove from heat.
Stir in vanilla.
Cool 20 minutes.
Stir.
Serve.
